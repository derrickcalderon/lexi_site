About Current CX

Built by a CX leader who's been in the trenches.

Current CX is owned and operated by Lexi Horn — a Customer Experience and Operations leader who has built and scaled CX functions inside both hyper-growth companies and organizations needing rapid cost correction.

After years of consulting alongside full-time leadership roles, Lexi launched Current CX to solve a common problem: most companies don't need a full-time Head of CX. They need focused, high-impact support for the moments that matter.

"The best customer experience is one where customers don't need to reach out. But when they do, it should be fast, clear, and effective."
