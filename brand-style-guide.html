Guidelines

  • ✓ Always use Inter Bold (800) for the wordmark
  • ✓ "CX" is always in Current Teal (#2DD4BF)
  • ✓ Clear space = 1× the height of "C" on all sides
  • ✓ Use on dark or light backgrounds only (high contrast)
  • ✗ Do not stretch, rotate, or skew the wordmark
  • ✗ Do not change the color of "CX" to anything other than teal
  • ✗ Do not add drop shadows, outlines, or effects
  • ✗ Do not place on busy or low-contrast backgrounds

Brand Voice

Voice & Tone

Current CX speaks like a senior CX leader giving a colleague honest, direct advice. Confident but warm. Specific but approachable.

Confident

We know what works because we've done it.

Direct

Short sentences. Clear outcomes. No filler.

Warm

We're on your side, not above you.

Specific

Real numbers, real results, real talk.

✓ Do

  • Use second person — "you" and "your"
  • Lead with outcomes: "Reduce churn by 30%"
  • Be specific: "Improved NPS by 20 points"
  • Use short, punchy sentences
  • Address pain points directly
  • Use active voice: "We build systems"

✗ Don't

  • Use buzzwords: "synergy", "best-in-class"
  • Use passive voice: "Systems are built"
  • Be vague: "We improve customer experience"
  • Use corporate jargon or acronym soup
  • Over-promise without specifics
  • Write paragraphs when a sentence will do

Copy Examples

✗ BEFORE

"We leverage innovative solutions to optimize your customer experience journey through synergistic partnerships."

✓ AFTER

"We help your team resolve tickets faster, keep customers longer, and spend less doing it."
